About Turi$mo

TURI$MO, a rising star in the world of hip-hop and rap, hails from the vibrant music scene of Ruiru, Kenya. Known for their distinctive lyrical prowess and unique storytelling, TURI$MO has captivated audiences worldwide with their raw and authentic approach to the genre. Drawing inspiration from the streets of Ruiru and the realities of urban life, TURI$MO's music is a powerful narrative of struggle, triumph, and self-discovery. Their breakthrough EP, "Akala Story," became an instant sensation, earning them recognition within the Kenyan hip-hop community and beyond. Following the success of "Akala Story," TURI$MO dropped "Rich Not Famous," a compelling exploration of the pursuit of wealth and fame in today's society. The EP's tracks resonate with listeners, offering a glimpse into TURI$MO's own journey and reflections. TURI$MO continues to make waves in the industry with their recent single "On My Face." This track showcases their growth as an artist and their ability to deliver thought-provoking lyrics over captivating beats, solidifying their place in the global hip-hop landscape. Notably, TURI$MO's talent has extended to collaborations, with a featured track titled "Stockholm." In this collaboration, they bring their signature style and lyrical depth to create a memorable and impactful musical experience. As a proud representative of Ruiru's burgeoning hip-hop scene, TURI$MO remains committed to authentic storytelling and a relentless work ethic, poised to make a lasting impact on the global hip-hop stage.
